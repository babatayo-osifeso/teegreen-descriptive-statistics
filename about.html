About Teegreen Descriptive Statistics

Teegreen Descriptive Statistics is a free online statistical calculator designed to help students, researchers, educators, and professionals analyze numerical data quickly and accurately.

Developed by Teegreen Tech, this tool simplifies descriptive statistical analysis by calculating measures such as the mean, median, mode, range, variance, sample standard deviation, coefficient of variation (CV), and Mean ± SD.

Our mission is to make statistical analysis simple, accessible, and reliable while helping users save time and reduce calculation errors.

We are committed to developing practical digital tools that support education, research, and data analysis while providing an intuitive and user-friendly experience.
